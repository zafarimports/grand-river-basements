How it's done

The 5-step coating system

No shortcuts. Proper diamond-grinding and crack repair are why our floors don't peel like a weekend epoxy kit.

STEP 01

Diamond grind

We mechanically grind the slab to open the concrete for a permanent mechanical bond.

STEP 02

Repair & patch

Cracks, pits and spalls are ground out and filled with structural polymer.

STEP 03

Base coat

A pigmented polyaspartic base is rolled edge-to-edge as the foundation layer.

STEP 04

Broadcast flakes

Vinyl flakes are hand-broadcast to full refusal for a dense, seamless texture.

STEP 05

Polyaspartic topcoat

A clear UV-stable topcoat locks it all in — walkable in hours, drivable next day.
