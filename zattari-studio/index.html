Case study 001

Grand River Basements

Live — grandriverbasement.ca

A basement renovation company serving Ontario's Waterloo region didn't need another pretty website — it needed a machine that never lets an enquiry slip. We designed an editorial marketing site and wired a complete pipeline behind it.

  • Every enquiry is captured to a Supabase database — nothing lives in an inbox alone.
  • The owner is alerted by email within seconds of a form submit, via an edge function and Resend.
  • Prospects book consultations straight into the calendar through an embedded Cal.com flow.
  • Local SEO structure targets five cities: Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, Guelph and Paris.
